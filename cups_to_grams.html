How to convert cups to grams

Depending on the ingredients makes the conversion from cups to grams more complicated than other units. So we're here to help you

This cups to grams converter can help you.

grams = cups x 236.588236 x material density

Where:

1 US legal cup = 240 ml

1 US customary cup = 236.5882365 ml

1 UK, AU, NZ, CA, ZA metric cup = 250 mL

1 c = 236.58823648491 g

Density depends on the material of the ingredients.

Thus, if the recipe reads 1/3 cup of all-purpose flour, and we know its density is 0.529 g/ml, we have:

1/3 x 236.588236 x 0.529 = 41.72 g
